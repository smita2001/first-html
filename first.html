FESTIVALS OF INDIA

Diwali or Deepawali is a festival of lights that is celebrated annually across India, especially in northern, western, and eastern India. The festival celebrates the triumph of good over evil.


Holi is a religious spring festival celebrated by Hindus, as a festival of colours. It is primarily observed in India and Nepal.

Lohri is a popular winter folk festival celebrated primarily in the Indian Subcontinent. The significance and legends about the Lohri festival are many and these link the festival to the Punjab region. It is believed by many that the festival marks the passing of the winter solstice.
